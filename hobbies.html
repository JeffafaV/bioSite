Beyond the Music

The hobbies that keep him curious

graeme's interests are as wide as his imagination. Each one feeds the creative personality behind the music — and most of them involve a little bit of showmanship.

On the decks

DJing

graeme loves to DJ, feeding off the raw energy of a crowd. Every set is a chance to read a room, take risks, and sharpen the same musical instincts that shape the songs he writes.

40+ costumes

LARPing

Live-action role-playing brings together his love of costumes, imagination, and friends. With more than 40 handmade outfits, it's not unusual to catch him running errands dressed as a medieval knight on his way to a convention.

Two weeks across the country

Traveling Japan

A recent two-week trip through Japan fed his curiosity and creativity — neon streets, quiet temples, and new sounds that find their way back into his music.

A personal challenge

Five-Star Driving

Driving for Uber pays the bills, but graeme turns it into a game — aiming to earn a five-star rating from every single passenger, one good conversation at a time.

The big dream

The world's first Bionicle museum

graeme dreams of opening a museum dedicated to Bionicle — a celebration of creativity, music, and the building toys that shaped his imagination.
