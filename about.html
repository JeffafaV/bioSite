Growing up, music was always a central part of graeme's life. That passion led him to study music graemery in college, where he developed the skills that would later shape his creative work. After graduating, he began writing and releasing his own music independently. While his songs generate only a modest income, several have gained a loyal audience on Spotify, making it rewarding to see listeners connect with the music he creates.

“Rather than waiting for the perfect opportunity, he enjoys creating music on his own and sharing it with others.”

Because pursuing music full time can be difficult, he works as a grocery store manager while also trading in the stock market. He continues searching for opportunities in the music industry but understands how competitive the field can be. That practical balance gives him the freedom to keep making the art he loves without the pressure of it paying every bill.

Outside of work, graeme has a wide range of interests that reflect his creative personality. He recently spent two weeks traveling throughout Japan and enjoys DJing, where he feeds off the energy of the crowd while sharpening his musical skills. Another favorite hobby is live-action role-playing, which combines his love of costumes, imagination, and time with friends. With more than 40 costumes in his collection, it is not unusual for him to run errands dressed as a medieval knight on his way to a convention.

To earn additional income, he also drives for Uber and has turned the job into a personal challenge by trying to earn five-star ratings from every passenger. Looking toward the future, he hopes to continue growing as a musician while eventually opening the world's first Bionicle museum — combining his lifelong appreciation for creativity, music, and building history.
